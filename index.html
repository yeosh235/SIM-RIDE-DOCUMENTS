About SIM RIDE

SIM Ride, also known as UOW Ride, is a web-based carpooling application designed particularly for SIM students and staff members. This non-profit platform aims to encourage ride-sharing to the SIM campus as an environmentally friendly and economical travel alternative.

Our Objectives

  • ✅ Reduce emissions of greenhouse gases
  • ✅ Lower transportation costs
  • ✅ Alleviate campus parking congestion
  • ✅ Enhance commuting experience for SIM students and staff
